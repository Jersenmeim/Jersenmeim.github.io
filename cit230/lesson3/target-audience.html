DESCRIPTION

‣ My target audience would be both males and females from ages 16 to 70. It can mainly use to anyone who is looking to find out about the weather. People who are booking holidays and wants to have a fun day out, wants to know the weather in different countries, or the weather at exact times.

TWO PERSONA

Persona 1

Name:

‣ Emma Miller

Job title/ Major responsibilities:

‣ 1st Year Student at Ateneo de Manila University

Demographics

• Single
• 23 Years Old
• Female
• Love’s Landscape Photography
• Outgoing Person
• Taking up a Bachelor in Science in Multimedia Arts

Goals and Tasks

‣ She is outgoing person, loves adventure and shows much patience. A cautious woman who prepares beforehand the things she’ll bring. One of her concerns is photographing in bad weather.

‣ Things she does while outside:

• Find a perfect shot of the day.
• Adapting in the situation while either the winds were too strong, rain is too much or it’s just a very sunny day.
• Finds a perfect location to shoot to and a place to cover up; if ever there’s an upcoming shower or heavy storm that soon will come.

Environment

‣ She always has data for internet usage, and loves browsing the internet through the phone. She seems to follow the appointment clock instinctively, thus a change of weather has a lot of impact for what she’s doing.

Persona 2

Name:

‣ Liam Jones

Job title/ major responsibilities:

Demographics

• Married

Goals and Tasks

‣ He has responsible attitude and able to utilize communication skills within a team. He always has phone in the pocket to see schedule and meetings. One of his concern is weather condition in the site and teaching appointment.

‣ Things he does while outside:

• Supervise safety policy in the construction site.
• Teaching Health and Safety procedures both private and in public areas.

Environment

‣ He is mostly outside, whether raining or in sunny times. He drives 1 to 2 hours from different meetings and construction sites. Thus, weather update is essential for him.

SCENARIOS

• I will take a picture tommorow morning, is the weather foggy or sunny?
